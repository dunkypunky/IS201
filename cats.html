Purr

The soothing sound of a cat purring, often associated with contentment and comfort.

Meow

The classic cat vocalization, meow, can express a variety of emotions and needs.

Hiss

A sound of defiance or defense, the hiss is a cat's way of warding off threats.

Chirp

Cats chirp to express interest or excitement, often when watching birds or other prey.

Chatter

The chatter is a rapid, stuttering noise made when a cat is hunting or watching prey.
